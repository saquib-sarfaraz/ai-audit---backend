# Economics

## Core Assumptions
- **Target Market:** B2B Startups / SaaS teams (10-100 employees)
- **Product Model:** Freemium utility (Lead Gen) feeding into a paid SaaS subscription model.

## Unit Economics (Estimates)

**1. Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC)**
- Target CAC: $15 - $30 per qualified lead (email captured).
- Early on, we rely on organic/SEO and community virality, bringing blended CAC closer to $5.

**2. Lead Value Calculations**
- We assume 1 in 20 leads (5% conversion rate) generated by the free audit tool will convert into a paid managed service or full SaaS product (e.g., $99/mo).
- Monthly ARPU: $99
- Assumed Churn: 5% monthly
- Lifetime Value (LTV): $99 / 0.05 = $1,980
- LTV:CAC Ratio: $1,980 / $30 = 66:1 (Highly favorable, typical for strong B2B PLG motions).

## Profitability Estimates
- **Fixed Costs:**
  - MongoDB Atlas: $0 (Free tier) -> $60/mo (Production)
  - Vercel/Render Hosting: $20/mo
  - Groq AI API: Extremely low ($0.05 per 1M tokens for Llama 3). Negligible cost for generating 100-word summaries.
- **Break-even:** The product reaches infrastructure break-even with just 1 paying customer. The primary cost is engineering time.
