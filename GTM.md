# Go-to-Market (GTM) Strategy

## Target Users
- **Startup Founders / CTOs:** Managing lean teams (10-50 employees) who recently noticed their software subscription bills skyrocketing due to AI tooling.
- **Engineering Managers:** Looking to centralize AI tooling (moving from individual ChatGPT Plus accounts to Team/Enterprise plans).

## Acquisition Channels
1. **Engineering Communities:** 
   - HackerNews (Show HN)
   - Reddit (`r/startups`, `r/SaaS`, `r/devops`)
2. **SEO / Content Marketing:** 
   - Blog posts: "ChatGPT Team vs Plus for Engineering Teams", "How much should a 20-person startup spend on AI?"
3. **Virality (Product-Led Growth):**
   - The shareable `/report/:id` URL allows founders to drop the link in Slack, driving their peers to the site.

## Startup Distribution Strategy
We will launch as a free "Audit Tool" to build a high-intent email list. The SaaS play is to later upsell these leads on a B2B platform that actively monitors their SaaS usage via OAuth integrations.

## First 100 Users Plan
1. Launch on Product Hunt as a free utility: "AI Spend Calculator & Optimizer".
2. Direct outreach to founders on Twitter/X complaining about OpenAI/GitHub bills.
3. Post a detailed case study on IndieHackers showing how we saved a mock startup $5,000/year using this exact logic.
